Shrimp Tacos Recipe

Description

Follow this recipe to make a yummy shrimp tacos. This dish features small corn tortillas, topped with fried shrimp, vegetables of different sizes and textures, and finally my famous shrimp sauce! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Raw, deveined, tail-off, medium-sized shrimp
  • Garlic clove
  • Canola Oil
  • Mayo
  • Sour cream
  • Lime juice
  • Sriracha
  • Garlic powder
  • Cumin
  • Avocado
  • Red cabbage
  • Red onion
  • Cilantro
  • Cotija cheese
  • Flour or corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Fry the shrimp after adding the spices and minced garlic
  2. Toast the tortillas
  3. Load up the tacos and enjoy!
