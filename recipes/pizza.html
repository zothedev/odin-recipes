Pizza Recipe

Description

Follow this recipe to make a delicious pepperoni pizza. The smell alone will make you think you're in Italy! Prepare your taste buds for the hand-tossed dough, the sweet and creamy marinara, chewy and flavorful cheese, and of course the meaty pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Canola Oil
  • Corn Starch
  • Tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Water
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • pepperoni Slices

Steps

  1. Mix dough ingredients to form a circular pizza crust.
  2. Mash tomatoes and add water, cilantro, and spices to create the pizza sauce. Apply on dough.
  3. Spread cheese evenly on the sauce.
  4. Add desired amount of pepperoni slices on cheese.
  5. Bake pizza at 450 for 10-14 minutes.
  6. Eat and enjoy!
